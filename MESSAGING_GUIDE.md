## Deal Readiness Score Explanation

### What It Is
A standardized, data-driven assessment of business acquisition readiness across five dimensions.

### The Five Dimensions
1. **Operational Clarity** - How well-documented and repeatable are business processes?
2. **Financial Transparency** - How predictable and sustainable is revenue?
3. **Owner Dependency** - How transferable is the business if the owner leaves?
4. **Transition Complexity** - What integration and handoff challenges exist?
5. **Risk Exposure** - What regulatory, customer, and market risks are present?

### The Score
- **80-100:** Highly Ready - Minimal risk, strong acquisition candidate
- **60-79:** Ready - Moderate risk, manageable with planning
- **40-59:** Moderate Risk - Significant challenges, requires mitigation
- **0-39:** High Risk - Major concerns, not recommended for acquisition

### Why It Matters
The Deal Readiness Score is to SMB acquisitions what credit scores are to lending — a standardized signal that enables faster, more confident decisions.
